The Challenge

87% of Government AI Models Never Reach Production

Building a working AI model is just the beginning. Most agencies struggle to move models from notebooks to production -- and even more struggle to keep them running reliably while maintaining FedRAMP compliance, ATO requirements, and continuous monitoring mandates.

Without mature MLOps practices, agencies face manual deployment errors, undetected model drift, compliance gaps, and an inability to reproduce results for audit -- all of which undermine public trust in AI systems.

Without MLOps:

  • ✕ Manual, error-prone deployment processes
  • ✕ No versioning of models, data, or configurations
  • ✕ Models degrade without anyone noticing
  • ✕ Cannot reproduce past results for audit
  • ✕ No compliance trail for ATO or FedRAMP reviews
Our Approach

Four-Phase MLOps Implementation

A structured methodology that builds MLOps maturity incrementally -- from manual processes to fully automated, self-healing pipelines with government-grade security at every layer.

Phase 1

Assess & Baseline

Evaluate current ML practices, infrastructure maturity, and compliance posture. Establish your MLOps maturity baseline against government and industry benchmarks.

  • MLOps maturity assessment (Level 0-3)
  • Infrastructure and toolchain audit
  • Security and compliance gap analysis
  • Current model inventory and risk profiling
Phase 2

Architecture & Design

Design MLOps architecture that meets FedRAMP, IL4/IL5, and agency-specific security requirements while enabling rapid model iteration.

  • Platform selection and architecture design
  • CI/CD pipeline blueprints for ML workflows
  • Security-first data and model governance
  • Feature store and model registry design
Phase 3

Build & Automate

Implement pipelines, monitoring infrastructure, and automation within your authorized environment -- with full audit logging and compliance controls.

  • Automated training and deployment pipelines
  • Model monitoring and drift detection
  • Automated testing and validation gates
  • Compliance-ready audit trail integration
Phase 4

Operate & Optimize

Transition to steady-state operations with continuous improvement, automated retraining triggers, and self-healing capabilities.

  • Runbook and SOP documentation
  • Team training and knowledge transfer
  • Continuous optimization and cost management
  • Quarterly maturity reassessment
Capabilities

MLOps Platform Components

Every component is designed for government compliance -- with encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and full audit logging.

Model Registry & Versioning

Track every version of models, data, and code with full reproducibility. Immutable audit trails satisfy ATO requirements and enable rollback to any prior state.

Automated ML Pipelines

End-to-end automation for data processing, feature engineering, model training, and deployment -- with scheduled retraining triggers based on data drift thresholds.

Secure Model Deployment

Deploy models with containerized isolation, canary releases, A/B testing, and automated rollback -- all within your FedRAMP-authorized boundary.

Drift Detection & Monitoring

Continuous monitoring for data drift, concept drift, and performance degradation with automated alerting and retraining triggers before issues impact decisions.

Feature Store

Centralized, governed feature repository ensuring consistency between training and serving -- with access controls, lineage tracking, and point-in-time correctness.

Testing & Compliance Gates

Automated model validation, data quality checks, bias testing, and security scanning -- with compliance gates that prevent non-conforming models from reaching production.
