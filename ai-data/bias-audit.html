The Risk

Bias in Government AI

AI systems can perpetuate and amplify existing biases, leading to unfair treatment of protected groups. In government, this means real harm to real people‚Äîdenied benefits, wrongful investigations, unequal services.

Common Sources of AI Bias

  • Historical discrimination in training data
  • Underrepresentation of certain groups
  • Proxy variables that correlate with protected classes
  • Feedback loops that reinforce bias
