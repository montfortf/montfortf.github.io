Operating Models

CoE Structure Options

1

Centralized

All AI expertise and delivery consolidated in one team. Best for organizations starting their AI journey.

  • Strong governance
  • Consistent standards
  • Efficient resource use
  • May create bottlenecks
2

Federated

Central CoE sets standards while embedded AI teams deliver in business units. Best for large organizations.

  • Business alignment
  • Faster delivery
  • Scalable model
  • Requires coordination
3

Hybrid

Central team for complex projects, embedded resources for departmental needs. Best of both worlds.

  • Flexibility
  • Right-sized support
  • Knowledge sharing
  • Complex to manage
