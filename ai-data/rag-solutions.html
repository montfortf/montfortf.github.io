The Challenge

LLMs Don't Know Your Data

Out-of-the-box language models are trained on general internet knowledge, not your agency's policies, procedures, and program rules. They hallucinate confidently, provide outdated information, and simply guess when asked about your specific context.

For government agencies handling sensitive constituent data, inaccurate AI responses are not just unhelpful -- they erode public trust and can lead to compliance violations. RAG bridges this gap by grounding every AI response in your verified, authoritative content.

Without RAG:

  • Generic responses not specific to your agency
  • Confident hallucinations on policy questions
  • No awareness of regulatory changes
  • No citations or audit trail
Our Approach

Enterprise RAG Implementation

A production-grade methodology that goes beyond basic prototypes to deliver reliable, auditable, FedRAMP-aligned RAG systems.

Phase 1

Knowledge Assessment & Data Preparation

Comprehensive audit of your knowledge sources -- policies, manuals, regulations, case law, FAQs -- to determine scope, quality, and ingestion strategy. We identify data sovereignty requirements and establish secure processing pipelines.

  • Content inventory and quality assessment
  • Document processing, OCR, and format normalization
  • Optimal chunking strategy (semantic, hierarchical, hybrid)
  • Metadata enrichment and taxonomy mapping
  • PII detection and data classification
Phase 2

Architecture & Retrieval Engineering

Design the retrieval pipeline with hybrid search (vector + keyword + metadata filtering), re-ranking models, and multi-stage retrieval to maximize relevance and minimize noise.

  • Vector database selection (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector)
  • Embedding model evaluation and optimization
  • Hybrid retrieval with BM25 + dense vectors
  • Cross-encoder re-ranking for precision
  • Access control and role-based retrieval filters
Phase 3

Generation & Quality Assurance

Configure the generation layer with prompt engineering, citation extraction, hallucination guardrails, and answer verification to ensure every response is grounded and traceable.

  • Prompt templates with citation requirements
  • Hallucination detection and answer verification
  • Confidence scoring and fallback strategies
  • Evaluation dataset creation and benchmarking
  • Red-teaming and adversarial testing
Phase 4

Deployment, Integration & Monitoring

Production deployment with enterprise integrations, real-time content sync pipelines, usage analytics, and continuous quality monitoring to ensure long-term accuracy and compliance.

  • API development and system integration
  • Automated content ingestion pipelines
  • Audit logging and compliance reporting
  • Performance monitoring and drift detection
  • Feedback loops for continuous improvement
