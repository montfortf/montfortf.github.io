The Challenge

You Cannot Automate What You Do Not Understand

Government agencies invest millions in automation and modernization projects that underperform because they are built on assumptions about how processes work -- not data about how they actually work. Cases take unexpected paths, workarounds become standard practice, bottlenecks hide in plain sight, and compliance violations go undetected. A recent GAO report identified multiple opportunities to address fragmentation, overlap, and duplication across government, with potential savings in the tens of billions.

  • Documented processes differ dramatically from actual practice
  • Automation projects built on wrong assumptions fail or underperform
  • Bottlenecks and wait times invisible without data-driven analysis
  • Compliance gaps and process deviations remain hidden in siloed systems

What Process Mining Reveals

  • Actual process flows vs. documented processes
  • Bottlenecks and wait times in real data
  • Process variants, exceptions, and workarounds
  • Highest-value automation opportunities
  • Compliance violations and policy deviations
Our Approach

Process Mining Methodology

From event data extraction through continuous optimization -- a data-driven approach that reveals truth and drives transformation.

Phase 1

Extract Event Data

We pull event logs from your systems -- case management, ERP, CRM, email, and legacy platforms -- capturing who did what, when, and in what sequence. We build a comprehensive event log that represents months or years of actual process execution.

  • Event log extraction from Salesforce, SAP, legacy systems
  • Data quality assessment and cleansing
  • Activity mapping and standardization
  • Time-stamped event sequence construction
Phase 2

Discover & Visualize Processes

AI algorithms automatically discover process flows from the event data, generating visual process maps that show how work actually happens -- not how you think it happens. We identify all process variants and their frequency.

  • Automated process discovery and mapping
  • Variant analysis showing all execution paths
  • Interactive visual process explorer
  • Frequency and volume overlays
Phase 3

Analyze & Identify Opportunities

We analyze bottlenecks, conformance gaps, and performance issues. AI identifies and scores automation opportunities by volume, repeatability, and potential impact. We compare actual processes to designed processes.

  • Bottleneck identification and wait time analysis
  • Conformance checking against designed processes
  • Automation opportunity scoring and prioritization
  • Root cause analysis for process deviations
Phase 4

Optimize & Monitor Continuously

We implement improvements, deploy automation where identified, and establish continuous process monitoring to track impact and catch new issues as they emerge. Process intelligence becomes an ongoing capability, not a one-time project.

  • Improvement implementation and automation deployment
  • Continuous process monitoring dashboards
  • Real-time alerting for deviations and bottlenecks
  • Quarterly process health assessments
Capabilities

Process Intelligence Features

A comprehensive platform that combines process mining with AI to not just discover processes, but predict, optimize, and automate them.

Automated Process Discovery

Generate accurate process maps directly from event data, revealing how work actually flows through your organization. Discover all process variants, their frequency, and the paths cases take from intake through resolution.

Conformance Checking

Compare actual execution against designed processes, policies, and regulations. Identify deviations, workarounds, and compliance violations automatically. Essential for government agencies with strict regulatory requirements.

Performance & Bottleneck Analysis

Identify where cases spend the most time waiting, measure cycle times across process steps, and pinpoint bottlenecks that slow service delivery. Quantify the cost of delays in hours, dollars, and constituent impact.

Automation Opportunity Scoring

Variant Analysis

Proven Impact

Data-Driven Process Transformation

Process mining is evolving from static reporting to real-time intelligence in 2026, combining with AI and automation tools to not just discover but predict, optimize, and automate process flows. Government agencies are leading adopters:

  • Benefits processing -- cut application-to-determination time by 40%
  • Permit processing -- identify and eliminate bottlenecks in approval chains
  • Procurement -- reduce requisition-to-payment cycle by weeks
  • Case management -- optimize intake-to-closure workflows in child welfare

"We thought we understood our benefits determination process. Process mining showed us we had 47 different variants of what was supposed to be a single workflow. Cases were bouncing between units, sitting in queues no one monitored, and following workarounds that had become institutional habit.
