Our Approach

Responsible Predictive Analytics

Fairness by Design

Predictive models in government must be fair. We build fairness into every step:

  • Bias testing across protected groups
  • Fairness constraints in model training
  • Disparate impact analysis
  • Ongoing monitoring for drift

Explainability

Predictions must be explainable to staff, constituents, and oversight bodies:

  • Feature importance explanations
  • Individual prediction explanations
  • Plain-language summaries
  • Audit trail documentation

Human Oversight

Predictions inform human decisions‚Äîthey don't replace them:

  • Decision support, not automation
  • Human review for high-stakes decisions
  • Override capabilities
  • Appeal processes

Continuous Validation

Predictive models require ongoing validation and improvement:

  • Performance monitoring
  • Accuracy tracking over time
  • Regular retraining
  • Feedback loops
