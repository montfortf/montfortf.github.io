The Challenge

AI Systems Need Ongoing Specialized Care

Deploying AI is just the beginning. Unlike traditional software, AI systems degrade over time as data patterns shift, models drift, and the world changes. Most government agencies lack the specialized skills and 24/7 operations capacity to manage AI in production while maintaining compliance.

Over 80% of government departments experience data silos that create inefficiencies in AI service delivery. Without dedicated AI operations, models lose accuracy, compliance gaps emerge, and the value of your AI investment erodes silently.

AI Operations Challenges:

  • ✕ Models degrade without detection or remediation
  • ✕ Data drift goes undetected for months
  • ✕ Limited internal AI operations expertise
  • ✕ 24/7 monitoring requirements exceed staffing
  • ✕ Compliance and audit requirements intensify
