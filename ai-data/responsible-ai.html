Framework Components

What We Deliver

Organization-wide policy establishing AI ethics principles, roles, and responsibilities.

  • Core ethical principles statement
  • Scope and applicability
  • Roles and governance structure
  • Policy enforcement mechanisms

Structured methodology for evaluating AI risk levels and determining appropriate oversight.

  • Risk categorization framework (Low/Medium/High)
  • Impact assessment criteria
  • Review and approval workflows
  • Documentation requirements by risk level

Technical standards and testing procedures to identify and mitigate algorithmic bias.

  • Fairness metrics definitions
  • Testing procedures and thresholds
  • Bias mitigation strategies
  • Ongoing monitoring requirements

Standards for making AI decisions understandable to affected individuals and oversight bodies.

  • Explanation requirements by use case
  • Technical explainability methods
  • Plain-language communication standards
  • Appeal and review processes

Define when and how humans review, approve, or override AI recommendations.

  • Decision authority matrix
  • Human review triggers
  • Override procedures
  • Escalation paths

Procedures for responding to AI system failures, bias discoveries, or harmful outcomes.

  • Incident classification
  • Response procedures
  • Communication protocols
  • Remediation requirements
