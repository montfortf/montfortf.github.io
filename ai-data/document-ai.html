The Challenge

Government Agencies Are Drowning in Paper

Federal agencies manage over 13 billion pages of paper records. State and local governments receive thousands of documents every day -- benefits applications, income verifications, identity documents, medical records, and legal correspondence. Staff spend 60-70% of their time on manual data entry and document review instead of serving constituents.

  • Manual data entry is slow, costly, and error-prone
  • Backlogs grow while constituents wait weeks for benefits
  • Inconsistent processing across staff leads to errors
  • Legacy systems cannot handle modern document formats

From Paper to Structured Data

Document AI extracts structured data from any government document type -- automatically classifying, extracting, and validating with human-level accuracy.

Our Approach

End-to-End Document Intelligence Pipeline

A proven methodology that takes you from document chaos to automated processing in weeks, not months.

Phase 1

Document Assessment & Taxonomy

We catalog your document types, volumes, and processing requirements. We analyze sample documents to understand variation, quality, and complexity.

  • Document type inventory and volume analysis
  • Sample document quality assessment
  • Extraction field mapping and business rules
  • Integration requirements with downstream systems
Phase 2

Model Training & Calibration

We configure and fine-tune AI models for your specific document types, training on your actual documents to achieve maximum extraction accuracy.

  • Pre-trained model selection and customization
  • Custom field extraction training
  • Confidence threshold calibration
  • Validation rule configuration
Phase 3

Integration & Human-in-the-Loop

We connect Document AI to your case management, eligibility, and records systems. Low-confidence extractions route to human reviewers with efficient interfaces.

  • API integration with Salesforce, legacy systems
  • Human review queue and exception handling
  • Side-by-side document comparison interface
  • Continuous learning from human corrections
Phase 4

Production Deployment & Optimization

We deploy to production with monitoring, optimize accuracy over time through continuous learning, and expand to additional document types.

  • Production deployment with 24/7 monitoring
  • Accuracy tracking and model retraining
  • Volume scaling and performance optimization
  • Expansion to additional document types
Capabilities

Intelligent Document Processing Features

A complete IDP platform purpose-built for government document types and compliance requirements.

Document Classification

Automatically identify 50+ government document types -- applications, pay stubs, IDs, medical records, correspondence -- with 99% classification accuracy and automatic routing to appropriate processing queues.

Intelligent Data Extraction

Extract structured data from documents including names, dates, addresses, income amounts, and custom fields -- even from handwritten text, low-quality scans, and photographed documents.

Cross-Source Validation

Cross-reference extracted data with authoritative sources and business rules. Flag discrepancies, verify identity information, and ensure data integrity with confidence scoring on every extraction.

System Integration

Feed extracted data directly into Salesforce, case management, eligibility determination, and legacy systems via API-first architecture with pre-built connectors for major government platforms.

Multi-Format Ingestion

Process documents from any source -- scanned PDFs, photographed documents, faxes, TIFF images, or digital submissions. Handle low-quality scans and mobile phone uploads with intelligent image enhancement.

Human-in-the-Loop Review

Route low-confidence extractions to human reviewers with efficient side-by-side comparison interfaces. Every human correction feeds back into the model for continuous improvement.
