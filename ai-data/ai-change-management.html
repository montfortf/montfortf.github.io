Our Framework

ADKAR for AI Adoption

We apply the proven ADKAR change management framework, customized for AI initiatives.

A

Awareness

Build understanding of why AI is being introduced and what problems it solves. Address fears and misconceptions head-on through transparent communication.

D

Desire

Create motivation to participate in the change. Show staff how AI will make their jobs easier, not eliminate them. Engage champions and early adopters.

K

Knowledge

Provide training and resources to work effectively with AI. Role-specific learning paths ensure everyone has the skills they need.

A

Ability

Support the transition from training to practice. Coaching, help desks, and mentoring help staff apply new skills in real work situations.

R

Reinforcement

Sustain the change over time. Recognition programs, success metrics, and continuous improvement processes cement new ways of working.
