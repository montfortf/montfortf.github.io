Tool #1: AI Readiness Assessment

ai-readiness-assessment.html 5 minutes Assessment

Comprehensive 20-question assessment evaluating organizational readiness across four key dimensions. Provides a personalized score with prioritized recommendations.

Dimensions Evaluated
  • Data Readiness (5 questions)
  • Technology Readiness (5 questions)
  • Organizational Readiness (5 questions)
  • Governance Readiness (5 questions)
Features
  • Progress bar tracking
  • Animated score reveal
  • Visual score cards
  • Personalized recommendations
Why It Works
  • Provides immediate value before asking for contact info
  • Creates awareness of gaps they may not have considered
  • Generates qualified leads (users who complete are serious about AI)
