The Challenge

Transportation Is the #1 Barrier to Healthcare for Seniors

Millions of older adults miss medical appointments, nutrition programs, and social services each year because they lack reliable transportation. For rural and homebound seniors, the problem is even more acute — a missed dialysis appointment or follow-up visit can mean a hospital readmission.

Most aging services agencies manage transportation with phone-based scheduling, manual dispatch, and no integration with care plans. The result is underutilized fleets, last-minute cancellations, and seniors who simply go without the care they need.

Without Intelligent Transportation:

  • ✕ Missed medical appointments due to no-shows
  • ✕ Inefficient manual scheduling and dispatch
  • ✕ No care schedule integration
  • ✕ Limited fleet utilization and high per-trip costs
