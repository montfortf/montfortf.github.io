The Challenge

Complex Programs, Manual Processes, Missed Benefits

Eligibility workers face a tangled web of federal, state, and local program rules that change frequently and overlap in complex ways. Manual screening across Medicaid HCBS waivers, Older Americans Act programs, SNAP, LIHEAP, and Medicare means eligible clients routinely fall through the cracks.

Without a unified rules engine, caseworkers must navigate multiple systems and reference guides, leading to inconsistent determinations, long wait times, and benefit gaps that leave vulnerable adults without critical support services.

Without Streamlined Eligibility:

  • ✕ Missed benefit eligibility across overlapping programs
  • ✕ Manual rule checking across multiple program guides
  • ✕ Long determination timelines frustrating clients and staff
  • ✕ Inconsistent eligibility decisions across workers and offices
Our Approach

Four-Phase Engagement

A methodical approach to building an AI-powered eligibility platform that automates complexity and catches every eligible benefit.

Phase 1

Program Analysis

We catalog every benefit program your agency administers, document eligibility rules, and map the relationships between programs.

  • Comprehensive program inventory
  • Eligibility rule documentation for each program
  • Cross-program dependency mapping
  • Current determination workflow analysis
Phase 2

Rules Engine Design

Architect a configurable rules engine that encodes eligibility logic for all programs and supports automated cross-program screening.

  • Multi-program rules engine architecture
  • AI screening model design
  • Decision tree & workflow configuration
  • Data model for client eligibility profiles
Phase 3

Integration & Testing

Connect the platform with existing agency systems, validate eligibility logic against historical cases, and refine AI screening accuracy.

  • System integrations with state & federal databases
  • Historical case validation testing
  • AI model training & accuracy tuning
  • User acceptance testing with eligibility workers
Phase 4

Go-Live & Training

Deploy the platform with comprehensive staff training, parallel processing validation, and ongoing support.

  • Phased go-live with parallel processing
  • Role-based training for eligibility staff
  • Supervisor dashboard orientation
  • Ongoing rules maintenance support
