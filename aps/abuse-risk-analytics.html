The Challenge

Investigators Are Overwhelmed Without Prioritization

One in ten Americans over 60 faces some form of abuse, neglect, or exploitation — yet only 1 in 24 cases is ever reported. APS investigators carry crushing caseloads with no reliable way to identify which reports demand immediate attention.

Without data-driven prioritization, all reports receive the same initial treatment regardless of risk level. High-risk situations get lost in the queue while investigators spend equal time on lower-severity cases. The result is burnout, missed emergencies, and preventable harm.

Without Abuse Risk Analytics:

  • ✕ Equal treatment of all reports regardless of risk
  • ✕ Investigator burnout from unmanaged caseloads
  • ✕ Late identification of high-risk situations
  • ✕ No data-driven resource allocation
Our Approach

Four-Phase Analytics Deployment

A rigorous, transparent methodology that builds predictive models investigators trust.

Phase 1

Historical Data Analysis

Comprehensive review of past APS investigations, outcomes, and risk factors to identify the signals that predict severity and recurrence.

  • Multi-year case outcome analysis
  • Risk factor identification & weighting
  • Recurrence pattern discovery
  • Data quality assessment & remediation
Phase 2

Risk Model Development

Design and train predictive models using validated risk factors, incorporating investigator expertise and evidence-based research.

  • Feature engineering from case data
  • Multi-model comparison & selection
  • Investigator knowledge integration
  • Explainability framework development
Phase 3

Bias Testing & Validation

Rigorous fairness testing across demographics, geographic areas, and case types to ensure equitable risk assessment for all populations.

  • Demographic fairness analysis
  • Geographic equity testing
  • Disparate impact assessment
  • Independent ethics review
Phase 4

Integration & Training

Deploy risk scoring into existing APS workflows with comprehensive investigator training on interpretation and appropriate use.

  • Workflow integration & automation
  • Investigator training program
  • Supervisor dashboard deployment
  • Feedback loop establishment
