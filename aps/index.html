Federal Compliance

The ACL APS Final Rule Changes Everything

On May 7, 2024, ACL published the first-ever federal regulations for Adult Protective Services programs. Every state APS program must comply by May 8, 2028—creating the largest APS technology modernization mandate in history.

  • Standardized definitions across all state programs
  • Tiered response system with mandated timelines
  • 24/7 online reporting capability required
  • Fiscal and performance reporting to ACL
  • NAMRS Case Component data submission standards
2028
Federal Compliance Deadline

States that fail to modernize risk losing federal APS funding and facing compliance findings. The clock is ticking.
