Predictive Risk Models for APS

Machine learning models validated on APS investigation data to predict abuse risk, prioritize cases by severity, and enable proactive intervention before harm escalates.

Request a Demo See Our Approach
The Challenge

APS Operates Reactively — Investigations Begin After Harm Has Occurred

Adult Protective Services agencies respond to reports of abuse, neglect, and exploitation after they happen. Without data-driven tools, there is no systematic way to predict which individuals are at highest risk or which cases demand immediate attention.

Predictive risk modeling changes the paradigm — shifting APS from reactive investigation to proactive protection by identifying patterns that precede serious harm and surfacing high-risk cases before outcomes worsen.

Without Predictive Risk Models:

  • ✕ All reports treated with equal urgency
  • ✕ No data-driven case prioritization
  • ✕ Reactive investigation model
  • ✕ Scarce resources spread thin
Our Approach

Four-Phase Model Development

A rigorous, transparent process from data preparation through validated deployment.

Phase 1

Data Assessment & Preparation

Audit existing APS investigation data for completeness, identify predictive features, and prepare clean datasets for model training.

  • Historical case data audit
  • Feature engineering from investigation records
  • Data quality remediation
  • Privacy and de-identification protocols
Phase 2

Model Development

Train and compare multiple machine learning approaches on APS-specific data to identify the most accurate and interpretable model architecture.

  • Algorithm selection and benchmarking
  • Cross-validation on historical outcomes
  • Explainability layer development
  • Threshold calibration for risk tiers
Phase 3

Bias Testing & Validation

Rigorously test for demographic bias, validate predictions against real-world outcomes, and ensure equitable treatment across all populations.

  • Demographic parity analysis
  • Disparate impact testing
  • False positive/negative rate review by subgroup
  • Independent validation panel review
Phase 4

Production Deployment

Deploy the validated model into the APS workflow with real-time scoring, monitoring dashboards, and continuous feedback loops.

  • Real-time risk scoring integration
  • Investigator-facing dashboard rollout
  • Model performance monitoring setup
  • Feedback loop for continuous improvement
What You Get
