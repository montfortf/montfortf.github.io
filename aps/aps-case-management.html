The Challenge

Legacy Systems Can’t Keep Up with APS Demands

APS agencies across the country rely on disconnected legacy systems, paper-based intake forms, and spreadsheets to manage some of the most complex and time-sensitive cases in human services. Investigators lack real-time visibility into case status, and supervisors struggle to track compliance with ever-evolving federal mandates.

The ACL Final Rule demands standardized data collection, timely response, and transparent reporting — requirements that outdated systems simply cannot meet. Agencies need a modern, purpose-built platform that unifies case data and automates compliance workflows.

Without Modern Case Management:

  • ✕ Fragmented case data across disconnected systems
  • ✕ Manual intake bottlenecks delaying response times
  • ✕ No real-time case visibility for supervisors
  • ✕ ACL Final Rule compliance gaps and reporting failures
