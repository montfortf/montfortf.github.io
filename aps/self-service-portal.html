The Challenge

Families Are Left in the Dark During the Case Process

When a family member reports a concern about a loved one, they have no visibility into what happens next. Case progress is invisible, document submission requires office visits, and the only way to get an update is to call — repeatedly — during business hours.

This lack of transparency erodes trust, overwhelms phone lines with status inquiry calls, and frustrates families who are already in distress. Caseworkers spend valuable investigation time returning calls instead of protecting vulnerable adults.

Without a Self-Service Portal:

  • ✕ Repeated phone calls for status updates
  • ✕ No document submission capability online
  • ✕ Families feel left in the dark about progress
  • ✕ Community resources are hard to find and access
Our Approach

Four-Phase Portal Development

A human-centered design process that builds trust through transparency and accessibility.

Phase 1

User Research & Design

Interview families, caseworkers, and community partners to understand their needs, pain points, and digital comfort levels.

  • Family & caregiver needs assessment
  • Caseworker workflow impact analysis
  • Accessibility requirements gathering
  • Multilingual content strategy
Phase 2

Portal Development

Build a secure, accessible portal with case status tracking, document upload, resource directories, and notification preferences.

  • Secure authentication & identity verification
  • Case status dashboard development
  • Document upload & management system
  • Community resource directory integration
Phase 3

Accessibility Testing

Rigorous testing with assistive technologies, screen readers, and diverse user groups to ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance across all features.

  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance audit
  • Screen reader & assistive technology testing
  • Multilingual content validation
  • Usability testing with family focus groups
Phase 4

Launch & Adoption

Coordinate public launch with community outreach, staff training, and ongoing feedback collection to drive adoption and satisfaction.

  • Staff training on portal-generated workflows
  • Community outreach & onboarding campaign
  • Feedback collection & iterative improvements
  • Adoption metrics tracking & optimization
What You Get

Portal Deliverables

A complete self-service ecosystem that serves families while reducing staff burden.

Family Portal

Secure, personalized dashboard where families can view case milestones, read status updates, and communicate with their assigned caseworker.

Case Status Tracker

Visual timeline showing case progress from intake through resolution, with milestone notifications and estimated next steps.

Document Upload System

Secure document submission with support for photos, PDFs, and forms — eliminating the need for in-person visits or faxes.

Community Resource Directory

Why It Matters

Transparency Builds Trust Between Families & Agencies

A self-service portal transforms the family experience from opaque and frustrating to transparent and empowering:

  • ✓ Give families transparency into case progress — real-time visibility reduces anxiety and builds confidence
  • ✓ Reduce call volume for staff — caseworkers spend time investigating, not returning status calls
  • ✓ Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility — serve all families regardless of ability or language
  • ✓ Connect families to community resources — integrated directory links families to services they need
