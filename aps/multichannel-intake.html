The Challenge

Most APS Programs Can’t Accept Reports Around the Clock

The ACL Final Rule mandates 24/7 online reporting capability by 2028, yet the majority of APS programs still rely on business-hours phone intake. Reports that come in after hours — often the most urgent — go unanswered until the next business day.

Meeting this mandate requires more than a web form. It demands intelligent screening that triages risk in real time, routes emergencies immediately, and creates structured case records automatically — across every channel reporters choose to use.

Without Multi-Channel Intake:

  • ✕ Reports limited to business hours only
  • ✕ No online reporting capability for the public
  • ✕ Manual screening overwhelms intake staff
  • ✕ Inconsistent risk classification across workers
Our Approach

Four-Phase Intake Transformation

A structured rollout that meets ACL mandates while minimizing disruption to current operations.

Phase 1

Channel Assessment

Evaluate your current intake operations, report volumes, peak hours, and channel preferences to design the optimal multi-channel strategy.

  • Current intake volume & pattern analysis
  • Reporter demographic & channel preference study
  • After-hours report gap quantification
  • Technology infrastructure review
Phase 2

Intake Workflow Design

Design intelligent intake workflows with branching logic, risk scoring algorithms, and automated routing rules tailored to your jurisdiction.

  • Screening question flow & branching logic
  • Risk classification algorithm design
  • Auto-routing rules for emergencies
  • Duplicate detection & case matching logic
Phase 3

AI Integration & Testing

Integrate AI screening across all channels, train models on historical intake data, and rigorously test with real-world scenarios.

  • AI model training on historical reports
  • Natural language processing for narrative intake
  • Cross-channel consistency testing
  • User acceptance testing with intake staff
Phase 4

Phased Channel Rollout

Launch channels incrementally — web first, then mobile, then enhanced phone — with monitoring and optimization at each stage.

  • Web portal launch with public outreach
  • Mobile reporting app deployment
  • Phone system AI enhancement
  • Performance monitoring & optimization

Why It Matters

Every Unreported Case Is a Vulnerable Adult at Risk

Multi-channel intake isn’t just about compliance — it’s about removing every barrier between a concerned person and a vulnerable adult who needs help:

  • ✓ Meet ACL 24/7 reporting mandate — achieve compliance well ahead of the 2028 deadline
  • ✓ Capture reports that would otherwise go unreported — after-hours, anonymous, and non-English speakers
  • ✓ Classify risk consistently — AI-driven scoring eliminates worker-to-worker variation
  • ✓ Free intake staff for complex cases — let AI handle routine screening so workers focus on judgment calls
