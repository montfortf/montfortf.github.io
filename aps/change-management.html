The Challenge

Technology Fails When People Aren’t Ready

70% of technology implementations fail due to poor change management. Public sector agencies face unique challenges—civil service rules, union agreements, and deeply established workflows make adoption even harder than in the private sector.

When APS programs introduce new case management systems or AI-powered tools without structured change management, the result is resistance, workarounds, and wasted investment. Success requires addressing the human side of transformation from day one.

Without Change Management:

  • ✕ Staff resistance undermines technology investment
  • ✕ Union grievances delay rollouts
  • ✕ Low adoption rates waste budget
  • ✕ Knowledge workers feel threatened by AI
Our Approach

Four-Phase Change Engagement

A proven methodology built for the realities of government—unions, civil service, and mission-driven culture.

Phase 1

Stakeholder Analysis

Map the full stakeholder landscape including union leadership, frontline staff, supervisors, and executive sponsors to understand influence dynamics and resistance points.

  • Stakeholder influence mapping
  • Union relationship assessment
  • Resistance risk identification
  • Organizational readiness baseline
Phase 2

Change Strategy Design

Develop a tailored change strategy that respects labor agreements, leverages internal champions, and builds momentum through early wins.

  • Communication plan development
  • Champion network design
  • Union engagement framework
  • Quick win identification
Phase 3

Implementation Support

Execute the change strategy alongside technology rollout, providing real-time coaching, resistance mitigation, and adoption tracking.

  • Champion network activation
  • Frontline coaching and support
  • Resistance intervention protocols
  • Adoption metrics tracking
Phase 4

Sustainment & Optimization

Ensure adoption sticks beyond go-live with reinforcement strategies, continuous feedback loops, and long-term culture shift support.

  • Post-launch adoption reinforcement
  • Feedback loop implementation
  • Success story amplification
  • Long-term culture shift support
